# What is the SJSU Purity Test?

The SJSU Purity Test is the first ever purity test designed specifically for San Jose State University students. Serving as a way for students to bond and track their experiences throughout their time at SJSU, this test captures the unique culture, traditions, and memorable moments that define Spartan life.

<Note>
Inspired by similar university purity tests, the SJSU version was created to celebrate the distinct experiences that come with being a Spartan.
</Note>

## Who Should Take the Test?

The SJSU Purity Test is designed for:

- **Current SJSU students** — See how your college experience stacks up and discover experiences you might have missed
- **Former SJSU students** — Reflect on your time at SJSU and reminisce about your Spartan journey
- **Incoming students** — Get a preview of the quintessential SJSU experiences that await you
