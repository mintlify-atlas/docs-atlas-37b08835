## The SJSU Experience, Quantified

The SJSU Purity Test is a community-driven web app where Spartans check off 100 campus experiences — from camping a study room at MLK to surviving the Panda Express fire — and receive a purity score from 0 to 100.

A higher score means you've led a more sheltered campus life. A lower score means you've truly lived the SJSU experience.

<Note>
  This is not a bucket list. You are beyond cooked if you complete all the items.
</Note>

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Read the questions">
    Work through all 100 SJSU-specific experiences. Check every item you have done.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit your answers">
    Click **Calculate My Score**. Your answers are saved to the community database.
  </Step>
  <Step title="See your score">
    Your purity score (0–100) is revealed. Higher is purer; lower means you've lived it all.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Compare and share">
    Copy a shareable result to send to your Spartan friends, or browse the live community stats.
  </Step>
</Steps>
