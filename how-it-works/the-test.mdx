The SJSU Purity Test consists of exactly **100 questions** covering every aspect of life as a San Jose State student. Each question asks whether you've experienced something specific during your time at SJSU.

## How Questions Work

<Steps>
  <Step title="Read each question">
    Questions cover experiences ranging from academic life to social activities, campus facilities to romance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the box if true">
    If you've done what the question describes, click the checkbox. Be honest—this is anonymous!
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit and get your score">
    After answering all 100 questions, click "Calculate My Score" to see your purity rating.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Question Categories

The 100 questions are organized into these main categories:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Academics" icon="book-open">
    Questions about classes, studying, professors, and academic experiences like skipping lectures, using AI tools, or pulling all-nighters.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Campus Life & Facilities" icon="building">
    Experiences with campus buildings like the MLK Library, SRAC, Student Union, and dorm life.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Dining & Food" icon="utensils">
    All the campus food spots: Panda Express, Halal Shack, Ginger Market, Tea Degree, and the infamous dining hall.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Social Life & Greek Life" icon="users">
    Student organizations, frat parties, campus parties, and Greek life experiences.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Commuting & Transportation" icon="car">
    The struggle of parking, scooters, and getting around campus as a commuter.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Campus Events & Community" icon="calendar">
    SpartanFest, career fairs, football games, and other SJSU traditions.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Romance & Relationships" icon="heart">
    Dating on campus, holding hands, and relationships with fellow Spartans.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is this anonymous?">
    Yes! Your responses are completely anonymous. No personal information is collected or stored with your answers. Only your score and checked items are saved to the community database.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I take it more than once?">
    Absolutely! You can retake the test as many times as you want. Each submission adds to the community statistics, but no individual is tracked.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens after I submit?">
    Your score is calculated and displayed on the results page. Your answers are anonymously saved to contribute to the community statistics. You can then share your score or view how you compare to other Spartans.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
