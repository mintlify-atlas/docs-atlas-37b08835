# Take the SJSU Purity Test

Taking the SJSU Purity Test takes just a few minutes. Follow the steps below to get your score and see how you compare to other Spartans.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Test">
    Go to the SJSU Purity Test website. You'll see the official test header and introduction explaining the purpose of the test.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Read the Caution Notice">
    Before you begin, you'll see an important warning:

    <Warning>
    Caution: This is not a bucket list. You are beyond cooked if you complete all the items on this list.
    </Warning>

    This test measures experiences you've had — it's not a checklist of things to do. A lower score means you've experienced more at SJSU.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check Off Your Experiences">
    The test contains **100 questions** about SJSU experiences. For each item, click the checkbox if you've done it. Questions cover topics like:

    - Academic experiences (classes, professors, assignments)
    - Campus life (dorms, dining, the MLK Library)
    - Social experiences (organizations, parties, relationships)
    - Local spots (Sparcos, Halal Shack, downtown San Jose)

    <Tip>
    Scroll through all 100 questions and check off every experience you've had. Don't worry about the order — just mark what applies to you.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Calculate Your Score">
    Once you've checked off all your experiences, click the **Calculate My Score** button at the bottom of the page.

    Your purity score is calculated using this formula:

    ```typescript
    const score = Math.round(((questions.length - checkedCount) / questions.length) * 100);
    ```

    With 100 total questions, the math is straightforward:
    - If you've done 20 things → your score is 80
    - If you've done 50 things → your score is 50
    - If you've done 0 things → your score is 100 (purest!)

    <Info>
    **Higher score = purer.** A score of 100 means you haven't done any of the listed experiences, while a lower score means you've had more SJSU experiences.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="View Your Results">
    After calculating, you'll be taken to the results page showing your SJSU Purity Score prominently displayed.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Share or Explore Stats">
    From the results page, you can:

    - **Share Result** — Copies your score and a link to the test to your clipboard, ready to paste anywhere
    - **Take the test again** — Retake the test if you want to double-check your answers
    - **View Statistics** — See how your score compares to others and which experiences are most common among SJSU students
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
When you share your result, it copies text like "I got 65 on the SJSU Purity Test! Try it yourself:" along with the website link. Paste it on social media, in group chats, or anywhere you want to challenge your friends.
</Tip>
